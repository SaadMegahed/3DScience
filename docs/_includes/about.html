Course Schedule

From beginner to expert in two weeks (IAP Weeks 2 and 3).

  • Jan 18, 2022 - 10:30-11:30AM

    The 3D Viewport

    The first class will focus on navigating the 3D viewport while exploring a small-molecule drug. By the end of the first class, you will feel comfortable manipulating the 3D work space.

  • Jan 20, 2022 - 10:30-11:30AM

    Compelling Lighting

    Lighting will make or break any composition more than any other factor. In this class, we will learn the basics of lighting while exploring an atomically-accurate protein or DNA.

  • Jan 23, 2022 - 10:30-11:30AM

    Scene Composition

    In this class, we will draw from the previous classes to compose a 3D scene and render it out as if it were a journal cover.

  • Jan 25, 2022 - 10:30-11:30AM

    3D Modeling

    Eventually you will want to build a more complex scene that contain objects like a beaker, eppendorfs, or a pipette. In this class, we will learn to import and work with open-source 3D assets.

  • Jan 27, 2022 - 10:30-11:30AM

    Movies and Animations

    In this final class, we will render an epic movie of two proteins binding with cinematic lighting and effects.
